SimplyRock

SimplyRock is a band consisting of eight skilled musicians and a sound engineer, most of them professionals, and it represents one of the leading tribute bands to Joe Cocker, the greatest voice of British blues/rock.

FAMOUS SONGS

In our concerts we play some of the most beautiful songs of the history of international music, from "Unchain my heart" to "You can leave your hat on", "Up where we belong", famous movie soundtracks.

LIVE SHOW

An exciting show, composed in its full version by 5 new computers that drive lights, action backgrounds, our live video with real time cameras, live streaming on Facebook and YouTube.
